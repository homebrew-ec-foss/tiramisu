> HSP brings you *BitFlip,* an interactive workshop on the *Theory of Encoding Data*, where we dive into the world of data compression and error coding, exploring the algorithms that power data storage, transfer, and modern communication systems.

![](/static/images/gallery/bitflip/hsp-bitflip-group.JPG)

### In this workshop, you will:

- Build a strong intuition for coding theory and error correction methods like Reed–Solomon
- Explore the limits of compression through Shannon's Source Coding Theorem and widely used LZ algorithms
- Dive deep into the inner workings of lossless compression algorithms such as LZMA
- Understand alternative compression methods like arithmetic encoding
- Get hands-on experience recreating and modifying compression algorithms using interactive demonstrations
- Explore techniques through interactive visualizations

![](/static/images/gallery/bitflip/hsp-bitflip-2.JPG)

![](/static/images/gallery/bitflip/hsp-bitflip-1.JPG)

### No prior experience required; open to both freshers and advanced users.

![BitFlip Workshop Banner](/static/images/gallery/bitflip/banner.png)

Bring your laptop, and we’ll take you from beginner to data-compression ready in just two and a half hours.
